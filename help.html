Using BizTrack: A Quick Guide

What is BizTrack?

BizTrack is your go-to business management tool designed with small business owners in mind. It's an all-in-one platform that helps you effortlessly manage your products, track orders, and stay on top of your finances. Let me walk you through the basics:

Navigating the Dashboard

The Dashboard is your central hub, giving you a snapshot of your business's overall performance. Here, you'll find key metrics like total expenses, revenues, profits and the number of orders. It's your command center for a quick overview.

Expenses Page

  1. Record Your Expenses: Head to the Expenses page to add your business expenses. Fill in the date, choose a category, enter the amount, and jot down any notes. It's that simple.
  2. Edit or Delete Expenses: Made a mistake? You can easily edit or delete expense records right from the Expenses page.

Orders Page

  1. Track Your Orders: On the Orders page, you can keep tabs on all your orders. Each entry details the product, quantity, and order status.
  2. Effortless Editing: Need to update an order status? Click the Edit button and make your changes.

Adding a New Expense, Order or Product

  1. Click Add Expense or the equivalent button on the order or product page.
  2. Fill in the product details, order details, or expense details.
  3. Submit the form and the new record will appear on the relevant page and dashboard.

Sorting and Searching Entries/Tables

  1. Click a table heading to sort records by that column.
  2. Use the search box at the top of each page to find matching records.

Export to CSV

Click Export to CSV to download a backup of your business data.
